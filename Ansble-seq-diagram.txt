participant Admin
participant Summon
participant Ansible
participant Database
participant PVWA
participant CCP
participant Conjur

Admin->Database:Provision DB w/ \nusername/password
Admin->PVWA:Create DB account in Safe \nsynced to Conjur
Admin->PVWA:Create AppId for Ansible, \nadd as member of Safe
Admin->Conjur:Create workload, grant \nSafe consumers role
Admin->Summon:Run Summon \nw/ Conjur workload
Summon->Conjur:Pull DB creds
Summon->Ansible:Run playbook that uses \nDB creds in env vars\nto access DB
Ansible->Database:Access DB
Admin->Ansible:Run playbook that uses\nplugin for CCP
Ansible->CCP:Pull DB creds
Ansible->Database:Access DB
